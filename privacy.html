Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 14, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the data handling practices for our content automation application ("the Application"). Operating as a secure conduit for publishing media to third-party platforms (including Meta and TikTok), we adhere to the highest industry standards regarding data privacy and security.

2. Data Collection and Processing

The Application functions strictly as an intermediary API integration. We do not collect, process, or store any Personally Identifiable Information (PII), user credentials, or media files on external servers. All authentication and data transfers are executed securely via standard OAuth 2.0 protocols directly between the user's environment and the respective social platforms.

3. Third-Party Services

By connecting third-party services, users are subject to the respective Privacy Policies of those platforms (e.g., Meta, TikTok). We do not share, sell, or distribute any technical telemetry or user data to unauthorized third parties.
