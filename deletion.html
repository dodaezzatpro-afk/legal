Data Deletion Instructions

As per compliance with global data protection regulations, EG provides transparent instructions for revoking access. Since our Application does not maintain an independent database of user records or media, there is no localized data to purge. However, to completely sever the Application's access to your social accounts, follow the official platform procedures below:

For Meta (Facebook / Instagram):

  • Navigate to your Facebook Account Settings.
  • Select Security and Login or Business Integrations from the menu.
  • Locate EG in your list of active integrations and click Remove.

For TikTok:

  • Open your TikTok Account Settings.
  • Navigate to Security and Login, then select Manage App Permissions.
  • Select EG and tap Remove Access.
